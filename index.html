Departments

At CSI Ebenezer Church, Vattakovil, we are blessed with a vibrant community of believers united in faith and service. Our church is home to various departments and ministries, each dedicated to nurturing spiritual growth, fostering fellowship, and serving both our congregation and the wider community. Whether it's guiding our youth, uplifting through music, supporting missions, or strengthening families, each department plays a vital role in the life of our church. We invite you to explore and become a part of these ministries as we grow together in Christ.

Our Branch Churches

Discover the growing community of faith through our branch churches, nurtured under the spiritual guidance of CSI Ebenezer Church. Each branch serves as a vibrant center for worship, fellowship, and outreach in its local area. These churches are not just places of gathering, but spiritual homes where lives are transformed, families are strengthened, and communities are uplifted. Explore more about each branch, their ministries, and how they are making a difference in the lives of those they serve.
