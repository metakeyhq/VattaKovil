We believe that no one should walk through life alone. Whether you're in need of prayer, carrying doubts, searching for answers, or simply looking for someone to listen—we're here for you. Life can be overwhelming, and sometimes all it takes is a single step toward connection to find peace and clarity. At CSI Ebenezer Church, we welcome you with open hearts, no matter where you are on your journey.

Please don’t hesitate to reach out at any time. Whether your reason is spiritual, emotional, or practical—whether you’re seeking comfort, community, or just a kind voice—we are honored to support you. Every message we receive is treated with respect, care, and confidentiality. You matter to us, and we look forward to hearing from you.
