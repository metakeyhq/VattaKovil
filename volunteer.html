Departments and Fellowships

Behind every song sung, every hand extended in kindness, and every prayer whispered in faith, there’s a ministry making it happen. At CSI Ebenezer Church, Vattakovil, our fellowships and departments are where passion meets purpose.

Each department is a chance to step into something bigger than yourself. No pressure. No obligation. Just a warm invitation to use what God has placed in you—for His glory and for the blessing of others. Whether you step in to help once or stay for a season, you’ll find joy, growth, and purpose along the way.


Join With Us Through the Following Ministries

Men's Fellowship.

Our Men’s Fellowship is a space for spiritual growth, brotherhood, and leadership among the men of our church. Through prayer, Bible study, and outreach, we aim to build strong, faithful men who serve their families, church, and community with integrity and purpose.

Women's Fellowship.

The Women’s Fellowship unites women across generations for worship, support, and service. By sharing faith journeys and ministering through prayer, compassion, and charitable efforts, this group empowers women to be Christ-centered leaders in both the church and society.

Choir.

Our Choir leads the congregation in meaningful worship, offering their voices in praise and adoration. With a heart for music ministry, they enrich every service through hymns and spiritual songs, glorifying God and uplifting the hearts of His people.

Media Department.

The Media Department supports the digital and visual needs of the church. From live-streaming services to creating graphics and managing audio/visual systems, this team ensures that our worship and message reach both our local and global communities effectively.

Youth Boys.

This fellowship nurtures young boys in their walk with Christ, shaping them into responsible, faith-filled individuals. Through activities, mentorship, and teaching, we encourage them to grow spiritually, mentally, and socially as they prepare for the future.

Youth Girls.

The Youth Girls group is a vibrant community where young women are mentored to grow in faith, character, and purpose. Through fellowship, Bible study, and service projects, they are guided to become strong, godly women in today’s world.

Sunday School.

Our Sunday School lays the foundation of faith in the hearts of children. Through Bible stories, songs, and interactive learning, we guide the next generation to know Jesus personally and live out His teachings from a young age.

Bible Class.

Bible Class offers deeper spiritual nourishment through regular, structured study of Scripture. It’s open to all ages and encourages a stronger understanding of the Word, helping members apply biblical truth in their everyday lives.

Education & Training Department.

This department focuses on equipping members with knowledge and skills to serve effectively. From leadership workshops to Christian education seminars, it nurtures growth in faith, service, and community involvement.

Social Welfare Department.

With a heart for service, this department reaches out to those in need through charitable programs, support initiatives, and community development. It reflects Christ’s love by meeting practical needs and restoring hope in challenging times.

Missionary Department.

The Missionary Department is committed to spreading the Gospel both locally and beyond. Through evangelism, outreach events, and mission trips, we share the message of Jesus and support the spiritual growth of new believers.

Medical Outreach Program.

This program combines faith and compassion by providing basic healthcare services and awareness programs to the underserved. It is an expression of God’s healing love, offering support to both the body and soul.
